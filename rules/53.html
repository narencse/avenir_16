Free-Wheeling

SINGING

1. You can perform in solo or in groups.
2.The performances include stand-up comedy, dance, singing, drama and recitation.
3. Guitarists are not allowed to used overdrive and distortion. And keyboardists are not allowed to use any tone from keyboard's memory.
4.Time is 10 minutes for performance and 2 minutes for preparation.
5. No extra time will be given at any cost.
6. Taking extra time will result as deduction of marks.
7. Judges' decision is final and irrevocable.

DANCING

1. All dance forms are allowed from classical , bollywood to salsa,hiphop with proper costume of that form.
2. Regional dance forms are also allowed.
3. Participants can perform for solo and groups. Member of groups are limited to maximum 6 people.
4. Time duration is maximum 10 minutes per candidate/group.
5. There will remain a theme on which the participants need to perform. If the performance gets distracted from the themes concept then he/she will be disqualified.
7. The music should be submitted to the organising committee prior to the competition.
6.  Participants must perform on the given themes in the preliminary round and only the selected candidates can perform on the final round.
7. Judge's decision is final.

DRAMA

1.Skits, One act character, One act, Short play allowed.
2.Maximum of 8 persons in a group.
3.Use of abusive languages or vulgar gestures will lead to disqualification.
4.Mics will be provided.
5.Music can be used only in the vocal.
