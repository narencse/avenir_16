Face-Painting

Here are the rules and regulations:-

Interested participants are requested to go through these rules:

1. Participant may draw on his/her own face or may bring model.
2. Participant must bring colours, brushes and other essentials. Stencils are allowed. Attachments of any kind are NOT permitted. No crystal diamonds. No string, elastic or tape.
3. On-spot registration is allowed or you may register online. Bring the money receipt with you on the day of the event.
4. Each participant will be given 2 hours to 2 and a half hours to complete.
5.The judge's decision is final. Don't wash your face without taking his/her permission.
