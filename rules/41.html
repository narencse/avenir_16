Click-In-Instant

A form of art can be either mutilated by a theme or it can be spontaneous! Do you know how to capture a moment in a frame that can bring out the essence of a festival Out here we will allow you to click throughout our campus all day long and by the end of the day we will anonymously select the winner and the runners up on the basis of excellence in creativity! Rules and Regulation: Any kind of camera is allowed. A person can submit only 3 photographs. This event will be held on the first and second day of Avenir. You will be given a theme just after registration. You have to submit your photo(s) on the day of registration only. No photographs of any other day shall be accepted. Selected finalists will have to produce their views and the ideas behind their photographs on that day. Judging will be anonymous.
