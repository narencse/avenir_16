Click-In-Instant


Lensified P2F in association with Cam2o Photowalkers presents "Click-instant" (a photography competition of AVENIR 2016(13-15 April)).
Theme:Will be given to resgistered participants during the first two days of AVENIR(13,14 April).
Submission :While theme will be given, the photographs should be submitted within 4 hours.
Submission place: NETAJI SUBHASH ENGINEERING COLLEGE
Registration fee: Rs 30/- per image.


Rules

1 Any student can submit their photographs in this event but he/she must be a student.
2 There will be only one winner in this competition. There is also prize money for the winner.
3 The photographs should be an original work of the photographer.
4 One participant can submit upto two images.
5 Only color photograph is acceptable.
6 You can submit your photograph in any format.
7 The submitted photo should not be edited. Otherwise your photograph will be disqualified.
8 Provide your name,phone number,stream year,college during the submission.
9 Those photos will be judged by professional photographers of Cam2o Photowalkers.
10 Result will be anounced and prize will be given on the last date of "AVENIR 2016" (15th April).
