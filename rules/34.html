Dispute

The competition is broken into 2 Rounds or Segments. They are –

1. Prelims

a. The Qualifying Round is held on both the First and Second Days of the Fest.
b. Every participant is asked to select a topic from a basket full of chits. The topics are as varied as “Shoe” or “Paper”.
c. Post topic selection, each participant gets 5-10 minutes for preparation.
d. Finally, each participant presents their speech, which should not exceed a total of 1.5 minutes.

Qualifying participants will be informed of their status and will proceed to the Finals.

2. Finals

a. The Final round consists of the main Debate which will be held on the Third Day of the Fest.
b. The respective Agenda will be made public at the end of the Second Day of the Fest. Please make sure you find out about it, if not explicitly informed.
c. The respective side (For or Against, the Motion) will also be informed with the above, for every participant.
d. On the day, an honorary judge will present an Introduction on the motion of the House.
e. The Dispute (or the Debate) will consist of Individual Orations from each participant for not more than 5 minutes.
f. Final decisions, rest in the judges’ hands.
Results to be announced at the end of the Third Day of the Fest.

General Instructions

a. Each individual gets a single chance to participate.
b. Topics for the Prelims cannot be changed.
c. A strict check on language is to be maintained. No slangs.
