Dispute

How confident are you about your standpoint How good are you at convincing opposing masses Do you think you have it in you to battle it out on the stage If yes, come show us. Let the war of words begin! Rules and Regulations: Every participant gets only one chance. Once a topic is given, no participant should ask for an alternative option. A student making use of offensive language will be disqualified. Oration will be of 5 minutes maximum Judges decision will be final Every participant should conclude their speech within the allotted time limit Rounds: Prelims ~ While event-registration, every participant will be given a random topic, which will be noted down, beside their name. Then, they get a maximum of 15 minutes to prepare their speech. The speech should not exceed 1.5 minutes. Finals ~ The 10 participants, selected from the prelims, will be given the final dispute ,the topic for which will be declared THE DAY BEFORE THE FINALS. One of The Judges will deliver a speech on the topic in general representing a general view. An additional 30 min will be given to the participants to prepare the speech. Their position regarding for or against will be chosen by a lottery system. The speech should not exceed 5 minutes.
