1. Please choose any ONE controller. Either Keyboard or Joystick. You can’t change your specified Controller midway into the Tournament.

2. There is no facility of Keyboard Vs Keyboard. Only arrangement is Joystick Vs Joystick Or Joystick vs Keyboard.

3. If there is any fixture between two keyboard users,both have to play against an admin . Else If decided by both players they can play against each other where both have to play one half with controller against the opponent.

4. Everyone has to bring their own Joystick. For keyboard players, they may use the given keyboard or bring it on your own.

5. Teams will be as per changes made after the winter transfer window. No players abilities can be changed.

6. Tournament mode will be knock-out.

7. Half-Time Length will be 6 minutes (each match),8 minutes (The Final, The semis, and The Third place match each of which will be of two legged draw) .

8. Players From outside of Netaji Subhash Engineering College will play their prelims on the second day of the event. All the finals including third place, semis, and the finals will be held on the third day of the competition.

9. Anyone failing to come up in the designated fixture of his, The opponent will get a direct walkover.

10. You can choose any team as per your wish(clubs and international teams only). There is no problem having more number of same team.

Prize Money will be decided according to the number of entries.

Prizes will be given to 1st,2nd and 3rd place holder only.
