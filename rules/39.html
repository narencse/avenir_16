Picto-Press


Here it is......"PICTO-PRESS", the story writing competition. We invite you all to AVENIR, the tech- cum management fest organised by PHOENIX, The official tech club of NSEC and grace this event with your precious participation.

Time to pick your pen up let your words flow like ink. A story writing competition to showcase your thinking level. .As it is said a pen speaks better than a sword !! So come up , join ,play with your words and let that hidden creative spark in you catch the fire :)

Rules and Regulation:

1. Some picture will be provided on the spot
2. Participants will have to link those pictures any order according to their wish and and frame a story
3. Word limit is 1500 words
4. Time limit: one full day time will be provided. Participants need to submit their write-up before 4 p.m.
5. Language can be anything Bengali , English or Hindi.
6. Top three writing will be selected..
