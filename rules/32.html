Rules and Regulations: The complete event is an on spot event and all the rounds would be held at Netaji Subhash Engineering College, Kolkata during Avenir 2014. Contrive is restricted to students currently pursuing their education in any recognized educational institute. A maximum of 3 participants can combine to form a team. Students from different colleges can be a part of the same team. The competition will be held in three stages Rounds : Round 1 ~ This round will consist of 30-40 multiple choice questions related to basic electrical and electronics. Each team will have to solve these questions in the given time. Round 2 ~ In this round each team will be given a task of making a circuit related to Analog and Digital Electronics. Each team will be given a multi-meter and a regulated DC power supply. Team will have to make the circuit out of the GIVEN components only. Before connecting the power supply, the circuit must be checked and approved by the judges. THIS ROUND TOO IS CLOCKED Round 3 ~ THE FINAL ROUND... This is the Group Interview Round in which each team will be questioned by the judges on the basis of the circuit made by the respective group. Judging Parameters : The winner will be selected on the basis of the score secured by the respective groups in Rounds 2 and 3 jointly. In all the cases the decision taken by the judges will be final.
