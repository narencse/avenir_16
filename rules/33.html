Quizzard

RULES AND REGULATIONS:

1. 3 member teams are allowed only.
2. There shall be a preliminary round from which the top 6 teams shall qualify for the finals. Both the prelims and finals shall take place on the same day.
3. Decision of the quizmaster is irrevocable and final.
4. Only students are allowed and appropriate ID cards need to shown during registration.
5. The prelims shall consist of 30 questions with starred questions so as to determine the qualifying team in case of a tie.
6. Cheating of any form shall lead to disqualification.
