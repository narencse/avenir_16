Con-2-Win


Con 2 win (also known as Antichess) is a chess variant in which the objective of each player to lose all of his/her pieces or be stalemated. Losing chess is one of the most popular of all chess variants.

RULES:

The rules are the same as those for standard chess, except for the following rules:-

Capturing is compulsory.
When more than one capture is available, the player may choose.
The king has no royal power and accordingly.
It may be captured like any other piece.
There is no check or checkmate.
There is no castling.
In addition to its normal promotion options, a pawn may promote to king.
Stalemate is a win for stalemated player.
When a player has only a pawn remaining, and the pawn is blocked from advancing, the opponent gets unlimited moves until the pawn is freed.
A player wins by losing all his pieces, or being stalemated.

Structure:

There will be a time of maximum 5 minute is given to each player.

1st day only prelims.

2nd day Qualifiers.

3rd day semifinal and final.
