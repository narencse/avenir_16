Knighted

Rules and regulations:

a) Time and league rules:

1. Each game will comprise of 20 minutes+20minutes ; 20minutes for each player)
2. The game will be in a rapid format, i.e. no need of writing moves down.
3. The game will be in a knockout format upto the finals.

b) Game rules:

1. Standard international rules will be followed.
2. Not more than 2 illegal moves are allowed. If a player makes more than two illegal moves, he or she will be disqualified.
3. Touch and move rule is there. If a person touches a piece, he or she has to move it, else he or she will be penalized.
4. Taking back a move is not allowed. If he or she wants to take back, he or she will have to forfeit the game.
5. Drawn on agreement is not allowed. Since this is a rapid format, there will no be no such drawn on agreement to be followed. However, in case of perpetual move draw, another match will be played between the players with 5 minutes each and if in that match, if there is still no decision, one whose time left is more will be the winner.

c) Rules for the final match:

1. Match timing: 10min+10min
2. Best of three matches.
3. If one match ends as draw, another extra blitz match will be played(5min+5min).
4. Same game rules will be followed as in other matches.
If any further inconveniences occur, the decision of arbiters and event organizing head will be considered as final.
