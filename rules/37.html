Its time we draw an ending line to the one handedness of Norway in chess... Magnus Carlsen is right now the undisputed champion of chess across the world. We will bring India to the top nations playing chess very soon. With the vision of making a few grandmasters in all fields we hereby introduce CHESS-MATE. Rules and Regulations: A multistage event, each being elimination round. Decision on both the defensive and offensive playing patterns of the player are tested at the same time. A player has to eliminate \'Soldiers\' of their opponents and earn points. Every \'soldier\' will carry a definite point which will be credited to the player. In case of draw, the judgement shall be on strategy. Time allotted for each game is a maximum of 20 minutes The two finalists shall fight each other till Check-mate The colour choice will be done by a coin-toss In case of draw, the total point shall be considered No points for castle or check. The reason is these are all tricks not pieces. One player can give infinite times of check to gain Points Board point will be considered only in case of tie in match or selection procedure. International chess rules are to be followed.Some relaxation can be made by the judge if it is required in case. otherwise Winning will always get the preference.
