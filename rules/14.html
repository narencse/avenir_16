Crypt-Quest

Hidden messages or Secret Codes ever fascinate you! Ever feel the urge to know what those messages actually mean! And work on it until you decipher the message! Then you are going to enjoy this Cryptography competition!

Rules and Regulations: Participants are required to solve the cipher text based on the clues given in the story line. In some cases clues have been omitted depending on its level of difficulty.

Participants can ask for the cipher that has been used, however this would lead to 50% deduction of score on that cipher text and also on all consecutive scores in the paper.

No clues will be given until half the time of the event has passed.

Participants are allowed to use any external source (internet, books) to solve the cipher text.

In case of tie, the time of submission will be considered to decide a winner.
