Rules

1 Anyone can submit their photographs in this event but he/she must be a student.
2 If your photo gets shortlisted for the exhibition , then we will inform you via mail.
3 If your photo get shortlisted, then you have to pay registration fee Rs.50 for each of your selected photos during the AVENIR 2016(13-15th April).
Judging will be done based upon the registered photographs.
4 Shortlisted photos will be exhibited during AVENIR 2016(13-15 APRIL).
5 The photographs should be an original work of the photographer.
6 One participant can submit upto three images.
7 Photo should be clicked with a mobile.
8 Everyphoto should maintain 1:1 ratio(Square format).
9 Black&White is also acceptable.
10 Photo should be insta edited.
11 Image should not be squared using any app ( i.e. "squaredroid").
12 The submitted image should not contain any watermark or logo or border. We respect your copyright, your work security is our first concern.
13 Rename your file with your name, college, year, and a suitable caption. (Example: The file name should be ‘Indranil_NSEC_3_sunset’)
14 Please submit your image in the above mentioned email address ‘instaclick2016@gmail.com’
15 Give your name,phone number,stream,year,college in mail.
16 There will be one winner and one runner up in this competition.
17 Those photos will be judged by professional photographers of Cam2o Photowalkers.
18 Result will be anounced and prize will be given on the last date of "AVENIR 2016" (15th April).
19 Failing to adhere to any of the guidelines will result in disqualification of entry.
