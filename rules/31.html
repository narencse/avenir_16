Inception

When you brainstorm, you create your own world of ideas.

Rules & Regulations

1. Team size: Maximum 5 members
2. The ideation will have to be presented by the participating teams in the prescribed format and materials as provided by the organising committee at Inception.
3. Ideation should involve engineering solutions.
4. Framework for Ideation proposal:

 Problem Statement Explanation
 Need for Solutions
 Feasibility of the Solution
 Challenges Associated with the Problem
 Proposed Technology
 Summary

5. PPT Pitch should not exceed 3 minutes and 2 minutes for Q&A.
6. The Finalists will be decided on the basis of feasibility and novelty
7. The finals judges will be playing the role of VCs. Each VCs will be given 10,000 tokens,which they can invest on best ideas.
8. The final round shall be a pitching round to the VCs, 2 min of PPT and 5 min of Q&A.
9. Finals will be judged on the maximum tokens earned by each teams.
10. The problem statements shall include everyday problems in the field of Healthcare, Education, Resources, to name a few.
11. The problem statements shall be provided at 10:00 AM at the event venue and the preliminary round presentation shall be at 03:00 PM of the ideas.
