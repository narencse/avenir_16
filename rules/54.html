Treasure Hunt

Rules and Regulation: Round 1~ A team can consist of maximum 3 members and minimum 1 member. A puzzle will be provided to you, solve it to solve the crossword within an hours time. Top 20 teams from the first round will qualify to the next round. Round 2~ Varieties of puzzles, cryptograms, brain teasers will be given to you, each lead to a clue or some specific item. Find the item, get it (BEG, BORROW, STEAL) but bring it along with you when you are coming up to match the answers. You must have the product with you or else correct answer of that puzzle will not be held valid. Round 3~ Top 5 teams from the second round will qualify for the third and the final round. In this round several clues will be given to you, you need to solve them and trace the path that leads to the treasure. The team who finds the treasure first wins.
