Treasure Hunt

The Treasure Hunt is an exciting event of solving a series of clues placed at various physical locations throughout the campus. Each clue will lead you to another clue and finally to the prize money.
Each team should consist of 3 participants.
The Treasure Hunt will comprise of 3 rounds.

1st Round: All the teams will be handed a set of puzzles. They have to engage themselves in solving the puzzles and 15 teams with the highest points will qualify to the next round.

2nd Round: The selected teams will be given clues and the first 5 teams those who will be able to retrieve the hidden item will qualify to the final round.

3rd Round: In this round the difficulty level will increase and the top 5 teams will battle it out among themselves. The treasure hunters will be given a list of items that they must retrieve within the stipulated time limit. One clue will lead to the next clue and so on and so forth. The team with the maximum number of items will be declared as the champions.
