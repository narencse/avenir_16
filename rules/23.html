Robo-Soccer

PROBLEM STATEMENT :

Design a manually controlled robot capable of playing soccer and scoring maximum number of goals against other robots avoiding different obstacles.

GENERAL RULES
A team may have a maximum of 4 members.
The members must bring IDENTITY CARDS of the college.
No person should be a member of two teams.
No bot can be shared by two teams.
The participants will be provided with 220 Volts, 50 Hz standard AC supply.
Participants will have to themselves arrange for any other power supply required for their robot.
Teams cannot tinker with their bots during the play offs.
LEGO kits or its spare parts or pre-made mechanical parts are not allowed.
 Any kind of damage to the arena by the robot will lead to direct disqualification.
No attack on the opponent bot is allowed.
Any act of misbehavior or misconduct will lead to immediate disqualification of the team.
The decision of the coordinators will be final and binding.
**The rules are subject to change.

ROBOT SPECIFICATION

The robot should have a maximum size of 25cm (l)*25cm (b)*15cm (h) with a tolerance of 5%.
The maximum allowable weight of the robot is 3 KG without any tolerance.
The robot can be powered on-board or off-board.
The potential difference between any two electrical points on the robot must not exceed 20 volts throughout the run.
External weights on bot are not allowed.
The robot should not grab/hold the ball at any point of time during the match.

EVENT RULES

PRELIMS
 The game will be of maximum of 5 minutes.
 The bot has to score maximum number of goals in a given time.
 Scoring goals from a specially designed position will be considered as jackpot.
 A maximum of 10 seconds will be allowed to hold the ball.
 The bot has to score goals from corner positions which can be done by shooting mechanism or any other way as per choice.
 While moving the robots have to avoid obstacles placed at different positions in arena.
 Two timeouts of 2 minutes each will be provided in case of any mechanical fault in the bot with negative points awarded for the second timeout.
 The teams with highest score will be selected for further rounds.

SCORING

 SCORE =50*[Number of goals scored] +100*[Number of Jackpot goals] +200*[Number of corner goals scored]-10*[Number of obstacles touched] -20*[Number of obstacles touched] -100*[Second timeout taken]

SEMI FINALS

 The game will be of maximum of 5 minutes.
 No modification is allowed with the bot.
 This round will be a knockout round.
 The team scoring the maximum goal wins.
 In case of tie, the match may go to extra time or penalty shootout.

SCORING

Whoever scores the maximum number of goals, wins .

FINALS

 This will be a knockout round.
 No modification is allowed with the bot.
 The game will be of maximum of 5 minutes in two halves of 2.5
minutes each.
 The bot has to score maximum number of goals against their opponents in given time.
 Bot will not allowed to hold the ball.
 In case of tie, the match may go to extra time or penalty shootout.
 The winner team will be selected for further rounds.

SCORING

Whoever scores the maximum number of goals, wins the competition.
