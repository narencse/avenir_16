Web-O-Tronic

Like designing web page?! Like to play with CSS and HTML?! This is about the web-designing event of Avenir 2016.

Rules and Regulations:

Team Size :
Maximum 2.

 Students may bring their own laptops and use any code editor for the purpose of designing.

 Time provided to complete the task is 4 hours. You can choose any 4 hour slot starting from 10:30 am. The competition will end at 3:30 pm (No extra time will be provided after that), so be present before 11:30 am to avoid any problems.

 Templates must be created from scratch. Use of any built-in template, template wizard, or template from the internet or any other source is not permitted, and will considered CHEATING.

 The topic for the website will be given On Spot with all necessary information.

 You can use custom HTML, CSS, and JavaScript frameworks or libraries from internet.

 Judging criteria:
Design: appropriateness, ease of use, visual appeal.
Content: quality and quantity of information, quality of writing and images.
Technical: functionality, performance (including page size), interactive features etc.
