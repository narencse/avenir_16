Bio-Scope 2016

Rules and Regulations.

• Time limit is 30+1 minutes.
• It is an open theme.
• Display of content or use of language which,if deemed vulgar/offensive by the judges, will lead to the direct disqualification of the participant.
• Films must be submitted in a compact disk or a pendrive on the day of the event, with the director and the film’s name written/attached to it.
• The video format must be compatible with VLC Media Player. And it has to be of minimum 480p quality, as it will be screened in front of the judges.
• The sound portion of the movie should be clear & audible.
• The movie should be an original creation of the team.
• A team should consist of a maximum of 6 members.(including the director, editor, cinematographer and cast).
• A synopsis of the movie is to be submitted along with the movie in that in doc file.
• The director will have to present an overview of the film in front of the judges & the audience before the movie is screened.
• Judges’ decision is final & irrevocable.
