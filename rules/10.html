Encoding

Rules and Regulations: There is an option between C or JAVA or C++ for Encoding and a team can opt for either of the languages. Each team comprises of 1 or 2 members. For the prelims there will be 30 questions and the time allotted is 30 minutes. For each correct answer 2 marks will be allotted. For each wrong answer 1 mark will be deducted. An online prelim will be held for the direct entry to the final round (much tougher) . Final Round~ The finals will take place in 2 rounds. In round 1 an algorithm will be given to you, you need to write the code for that algorithm and get the desired output. The first round will be a \'lock n key\' round where the output of the given program will be the key to unlock the immediate next question. In the 2nd round you will be given 5 algorithms.
