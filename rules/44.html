Spectrum


Lensified P2F in association with Cam2o Photowalkers presents "SPECTRUM" (a photography competition of AVENIR 2016) starting from 20th March - 5th APRIL .

THEME:
**COLORS OF LIFE**
PRIZE:
**AROUND 10K RUPEES TO BE WON**

THE RULES FOR THIS PHOTOGRAPHY EVENT ARE:

1. Event is open to all but the participant must be a student.
2. There are no upload limits.
4. The submitted photograph should be maintaining 2:3 ratio.
3. Only minor burning, dodging and/or color correction is acceptable, as is cropping.
4. No watermarks/ names or any other marks should be added on photo. Due credit will be given to the photographer if photo is selected for exhibition
B&W is also acceptable
Alteration of elements [addition, deletion, cloning, bluring etc.] and unnatural processing [including HDR & Selective Coloring] to be avoided. Adjustment of Brightness, Contrast, Color Saturation, Sharpening, marginal Cropping and limited post processing is acceptable.
5. Entries should be upload to ' SPECTRUM ' event page in with proper caption.
Link: https://www.facebook.com/events/1021124487930348/
Example -
Title of the Photograph as ‘Name_Photo Caption’ ie. If your name is Manasij Kanjilal, and your photo caption is “Smile”, then the Title of the photo would be
" Manasij_kanjilal_Smile "
6. Judging will be based on the merit of the photograph by a panel of professional photographers of Cam2o Photowalkers.
7. The photograph, in its entirety, must be a single work of original material taken by the contest entrant. By entering the contest, entrant represents, acknowledges, and warrants that the submitted photograph is an original work created solely by the entrant, that the photograph does not infringe on the copyrights, trademarks, moral rights, rights of privacy/publicity or intellectual property rights of any person or entity, and that no other party has any right, title, claim, or interest in the photograph.
8. Failing to adhere to any of the guidelines will result in disqualification of entry.
9. All decisions by organizers will be considered as final.
10. Participants agree to submit a 300 dpi and 2:3 maintaining ratio image if the entry gets shortlisted for exhibition.
11. If your photo gets shortlisted, then you have to pay registration fee Rs.100 for each of your selected photos during the AVENIR 2016(13-15th April).
12. Judging will be based upon the registered photographs.
13. There will be one winner and one runner-up in this competition.
14. Lensified P2F reserves the right to print the 300 dpi pictures and exihibits them during the fest days, i.e. 13-15 April 2016.
15. Last date to submit your entry - 5th APRIL 2016.
16. Result will be anounced and prizes will be distributed on the last date of "AVENIR 2016" (15th April).
