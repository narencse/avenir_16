Metal Mayhem

AIM :

Design and build manually controlled robots leashed with destructive weapons to harm and immobilize other robots in knock out rounds.

GENERAL RULES:

• A team can have a maximum of 4 members.

• No two teams should participate with the same robot(s) for this event. A team can have participants from different schools or colleges.

• The right spirit of participation is expected from every participant.

• The decision of the coordinators will be final and binding upon any participant.

• Every team should have a unique team name, keeping in mind that the name shouldn’t be offensive or inappropriate.

ROBOWAR RULES:
(NOTE:- Organizers reserve the right to change any of the rules if required.)

• The event is conducted in knock out rounds in which two robots will take each other in a bout.

• Lottery system will be used to select the teams that will compete with each other in a bout.

• Each bout will consist of 3 rounds.

• Each round will be of 10 minutes in which a robot will try to immobilize the other robot,after every round 5 minutes interval will be given.

• The teams have to initially place their robots at their respective marked corners, where the robot is to be placed. The starting position will be a square box marked on the arena.

• After the inspection of the robot is complete, the coordinator will signal the start of the match and the time keeper will turn on the timer.

• The winner will be decided subjectively as per individual robot scores.

• Further round details and scoring scheme will be notified soon.

• A robot that is deemed unsafe by the coordinators, after the match has begun, will be disqualified. The match will be immediately halted and the opponent will be awarded a win.

WAR-BOT SPECIFICATION

• Size: 50cmX50cmX50cm

• The maximum weight allowed is 35 kgs including on board batteries.

MOBILITY:

• Robot has to be manually controlled.

• Motion of robot should be continuous.Jumping,hopping, or flying is not allowed.

ROBOT CONTROL REQUIREMENTS:

• The robot can wireless or wired.

• In wired control the potential difference of the input in the remote should not exceed 48V at any point of the event.

• In wireless remote the input may not exceed 12V.

• Damage to arena will cause to instant disqualification.

BATTERY AND POWER :

• We will provide 220v ac supply for each team.

• The robot can have on board or off board power supply.

• On board battery must be sealed and ensure fire safety.

• All efforts must be made to protect battery fire,failing to do so will lead into direct disqualification.

• Use of damaged and leaked batteries is not allowed.

• Change of batteries will not be allowed during the match.

HYDRAULICS:

• Liquid used in hydraulic should not be inflammable.

• Maximum pressure should be 1bar.

WEAPON SYSTEM AND LIMITATIONS:

• Lifting mechanisms used should be properly fitted. Safety is should be the first priority.

• The robot should be capable of Pushing, and Crushing. (use of any blades, fire and other Potentially fatal techniques is not allowed )

• Cutting wire of the opponent’s robot is not allowed.

• No weapons used should come in contact with the Arena at any point of time.

• The safety of the bot will be judged by the coordinators.Their decision will be the final one.
