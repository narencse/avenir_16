RULES: GENERAL RULES: The maximum team size is 4. The participants must be students of same/different college(s). An individual cannot be part of more than one team. The participating team found guilty of damaging the arena in anyway will be penalized or even disqualified. The decisions of the judges will be final and irrevocable. Organizers reserve the right to change any of the above rules they deems to be fit. Arena can be change at any point of time of the competition. Participants must bring the valid identity card of their institutes. Robot Specification: The dimension of the robot must be 60cm x 60cm x 60cm at any point of the event. Maximum weight allowed is 30 kg including on board batteries. Weight of remote controller will not be counted. Mobility: Robot should have visible and controlled mobility in order to compete. Motion of robot should be continuous. Jumping, hopping, or flying is not allowed. Robot Control requirements: Robot can be wireless or wired. In wired remote control the potential difference of the input in the remote should not exceed 48V at any point of the event. In wireless remote the input may exceed 12V. Damage to arena will cause to instant disqualification. Battery and Power: We will provide only one plugin point of 220 v ac supply for each team. On board batteries must be sealed. All efforts must be made to protect battery fire, failing to do so will lead into direct disqualification Use of damaged and leaked batteries is not allowed. Change of batteries will not be allowed during the match. Batteries of bot should be on board only. Hydraulics: Liquid used in hydraulics should be inflammable. Pressure maximum allowed is 1bar. Weapon System: Robot can have any type of weapon system. Ex: magnetic weapons, cutters, flippers, saws, lifting devices, hammers etc. but it should be fitted properly. The safety of the bot will be judged by the coordinators. Their decision will be the final one. Limitations: Wire cutting not allowed.
