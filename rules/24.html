Terra-Ranger

Problem Statement:

Design a manually controlled robot that can traverse through terrains. The bot has to traverse through a path with hurdles like water, sand, oil, spiked path, rotating discs, inclined planes, etc in minimum possible time.
There may be broken paths in the arena where the bot has fill the paths by pushing only.

Team specification:

 The maximum team size is 4.
 Students from different colleges, sections, stream and years can form a team.
 Only one bot per team will be allowed and no two team will be allowed to share their bots.
 No person shall be associated with two teams.
 Use of Cell phone or Camera by the Participants at the arena is not allowed.

Robot specification:

 Dimensions of the robot max 30cmX30cmX30cm.
 The robot can use on board or adapter as a power source.
 The robot has to be manually controlled.
 The robots cannot be constructed using lego kits, or any such ready made products.
 In case of component failure or any other faults that may appear during the course of competition, the team can choose to rake a “technical time out” of 2 mins or restart ,but point would be deducted.
 Any modification of the robot is not allowed after the run has begun.
 Participants will be provided with only 220V power supply. You are requested to bring your own adapters an extension cords.
 The potential difference between any two points on the robots must not exceed 20V.Maximum current rating permitted is 2A.

Event rules:

 The bot will be awarded 1000 points initially.
 2 trials will be allowed.
 Points will be awarded for crossing each check point.
 Robots coming in contact with “touch not zones” will lose points.
 10 hand touches, 2 restarts are permitted only with deduction of points.
 In case of hand touch the robot will be placed at the end of the last check point.
 If a robot is unable to traverse a section within 3 chances, automatically they lose the points of that section. And the robot will be placed at the beginning of the next check point. Hand touch will be counted. Timer won’t stop.
 If bot or team members cause any damage to arena they are disqualified.
