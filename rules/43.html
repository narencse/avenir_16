pMovie, as a form of art can be interpreted with different thoughts. A Movie articulate is all about that! Many of us edit Wiki movie reviews & IMDB! Its time you place your bets into this competition & Show them what you got! This event showcases the talent in review writing!

Rules & regulation:

• The event will take place in two rounds.
• In the first round a short film will be shown to the participants & they have to a write a review within 250 words.
• From this round 10 participants will be selected for the final round.
• In the finals another short film will be screened and the participants will have to write a review within 400 words.
• Language can be either English or Bengali.
• Judges’ decision is final & irrevocable.
