Intrusion

Problem Statement:

The objective of this contest is for an autonomous robot to follow a black line on a white background, without losing the line, and navigating several 90 degree turns, four way crossings and intersections. The robot has to complete the course in the shortest period of time tracking the shortest course of line from start to finish wins. Only the time Matters.

Team specification:

1. Maximum 4 members per team.
2. Students from different colleges, sections, stream and years can form a team.
3. Only one bot per team will be allowed and no two team will be allowed to share their bots.
4. No person shall be associated with two teams.
5. Use of Cell phone or Camera by the Participants at the arena is not allowed.
Arena Specifications: 210cm wide by 240cm long

ROBOT SPECIFICATIONS:

• The robot must be completely autonomous. It should not receive any help from humans during the course of its run.
• The robots should have the capability to process data on-board. No remotely kept computer should control the robot.
• The robot has to be developed and designed by the team itself. The usage of commercially available sensors, development boards is allowed, but it is forbidden to use a complete robotic system which is sold ready for the purpose of the competition (like LEGO kits).
• The size of the robot must not exceed 25cm * 25cm * 25cm. Individual parameters to be measured.
• Robots or participants must not damage the tournament area.
• The robot may use on board battery, or using adapters of 9-12V, max 1A.
• In case of external power supplies, the wire must slag all the time, pulling wires to alter the movement of the robots WILL NOT BE TOLERATED at all and the responsible team will be
DISQUALIFED. The extension cords have to be arranged by the participants.
