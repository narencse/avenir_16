Rules

1 Only students of Netaji Subhash Engineering College can submit their photographs in this event. Those photos will be judged by professional photographers of Cam2o Photowalkers.
2 The photographs should be an original work of the photographer.
3 One participant can submit upto three images.
Black and White photograph is also acceptable.
4 Only pictures belonging to the specified theme will be accepted.
5. Only minor burning, dodging and/or color correction is acceptable, as is cropping. Alteration of elements [addition, deletion, cloning, bluring etc.] and unnatural processing [including HDR & Selective Coloring] to be avoided. Adjustment of Brightness, Contrast, Color Saturation, Sharpening, marginal Cropping and limited post processing is acceptable.
6 Image/Images should be in .jpeg/.jpg format.
7 The submitted image should not contain any watermark or logo or border. We respect your copyright, your work security is our first concern.
8 Rename your file with your name, stream, year, and a suitable caption. (Example: The file name should be ‘Shreyas_Das_CSE_3_smile.jpeg’).
9 There will be one winner and one runner-up in this competition.
10 Result will be anounced and prizes will be distributed on the last date of "AVENIR 2016" (15th April).
11 Failing to adhere to any of the guidelines will result in disqualification of entry.
