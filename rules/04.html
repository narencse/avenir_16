DOTA 2 (Defence Of The Ancients)

Dota 2 is a multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) video game and the stand-alone sequel to the Defense of the Ancients (DotA) mod. Each match of Dota 2 is independent and involves two teams, both containing five players and each occupying a stronghold at either end of the map. Located in each stronghold is a building called the Ancient to win, a team must destroy the enemy's Ancient. Each player controls a Hero character and focuses on levelling up, collecting gold, acquiring items and fighting against the other team to achieve victory. The Game was officially released on July 9th 2013, but now the game has become the most played on Steam, with daily peaks of over 600,000 concurrent players. So all Dota 2 gamers and aspiring players, this is your platform to prove yourself and show what you have got. Join the arena with us and unleash the battle.

General Rules:

Each team should submit a full list of its members prior to the beginning of the tournament. These lists should include all the members of the team.
The teams may include up to 6 players into the submitted list (5 general and 1 reserve players). Player can be replaced only before the match starts; no replacements are permitted during the match. Name of the captain must also be mentioned.
One player is not allowed to participate in several teams.
All the registered teams must have a specific name. This name must be provided at the time of reporting.
Usage of unfair means will lead to immediate disqualification. This decision will be taken by the organizers. Teams might also appeal if they feel that their opponents are resorting to unfair means.

General Game Settings:
Prelims~

There are 16 team slots available for registration (First come first service basis) Total teams will be divided in two groups after all teams are registered.(Gr A and Gr B)
The order of team matchups is random.
In each series, a coin toss decides which team has Selection Priority. The team with Selection Priority gets to either pick their side of the map or the drafting order.

Main Event:

The winners of each Group will go into Upper Bracket 1 and 2 respectively and losers move to Lower Bracket as same.

Upper Bracket 1:

Winners of each group A matches will come into this bracket and matches will commence as per schedule decided by admins. Top 2 Teams will move into the Quarter Finals.

Upper Bracket 2:

Winners of each group B matches will come into this bracket and matches will commence as per schedule decided by admins. Top 2 Teams will move into the Quarter Finals.

Lower Bracket1:

Losers of each group A matches will come into this bracket and matches will commence as per schedule decided by admins. The Top 2 teams will play against the 3rd and 4th placed teams of Upper Bracket 1 for a place in the Quarter Finals.

Lower Bracket 2:

Losers of each group B matches will come into this bracket and matches will commence as per schedule decided by admins. The Top 2 teams will play against the 3rd and 4th placed teams of Upper Bracket 2 for a place in the Quarter Finals.

Quarter Finals

The Top 8 teams will play each other randomly as scheduled by Admins. Such as Game 1, Game 2, Game 3, Game 4.

Semi Finals

The winners of Quarter Finals will play such as Winner of Game 1 Plays with Winner of Game 2- Tier1 Winner of Game 3 Plays with Winner of Game 4- Tier2

Finals~

The Finals will be played between Winners of Tier 1 and Winners of Tier 2.

IN ANY CASE THE DECISION OF THE ADMINISTRATOR WILL BE FINAL AND ANY TEAM NOT AGREEING TO IT WILL BE LIABLE TO DISQUALIFICATION. RULES ARE SUBJECT TO CHANGE TILL LAST MINUTE ACCORDING TO ADMIN\'S DISCRETION
